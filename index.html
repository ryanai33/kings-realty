Our Philosophy

We Sell By Price.
Not by Auction.

✦

Our methodology is fundamentally different from franchise agencies. While others ask the market what it will pay, we establish the true value of your property first — then find the buyer who recognises and shares that valuation.

"The majority of homes we represent sell for the asking price. Not because we're fortunate — but because we take the time to find the right buyer."

Terry King · Principal, The Kings of Real Estate

This patient, principled approach has earned the trust of Remuera's most discerning families for over four decades. We maintain a private register of qualified buyers — often meaning your home is sold before it ever reaches the open market.

The Process

How We Achieve Exceptional Results

01

Establish True Value

We conduct a thorough, unbiased valuation of your property — drawing on four decades of Remuera sales data and intimate knowledge of what discerning buyers in this market genuinely value.

02

Match from the Register

Before your property reaches the open market, we consult our private register of pre-qualified buyers. Many of our sales are completed quietly, with full discretion and without public listing.

03

Sell at Asking Price

Because we've matched the right buyer to the right property at the right price, negotiations are efficient and dignified. The result: the majority of our vendors receive exactly what they asked for.
